Bundled OCR models
==================

ch_PP-OCRv3_det_infer.onnx   PP-OCRv3 text detection (DBNet)
ch_PP-OCRv3_rec_infer.onnx   PP-OCRv3 text recognition (CRNN + CTC)

These are the PaddleOCR PP-OCRv3 inference models, exported to ONNX as distributed
with the RapidOCR project. They are used by the refinery extractor as a classical-OCR
cross-reader (a decorrelated second opinion on the numeric cells the VLM mis-reads —
see TextDetector.kt / DigitOcr.kt / PanelOcr.kt).
